Empowering community through arts, culture, health, and technology

The 1901 Project is a group of community leaders who are dedicated to enhancing the culture of their community. They operate as a creative agency and work with local government and businesses to provide valuable experiences and resources to community members. In addition to serving as a marketing and event partner, the 1901 Project also partners with other non-profit organizations to expand their reach and impact within the community. This collaboration helps to promote the growth and development of the community by providing access to a variety of resources and opportunities for community members to get involved. Overall, the goal of the 1901 Project is to improve the well-being and quality of life for those who live in the community by fostering a sense of connection and engagement.

About Us

The 1901 Project is a California-registered 501(c)3 non-profit organization that is dedicated to improving the culture of the community through education, arts, music, mentorship, workshops, and industry resources. In addition to these cultural enrichment efforts, the 1901 Project is also focused on financial literacy and helping to provide low-resource communities with access to new technologies. These efforts are aimed at empowering individuals and communities to succeed and thrive by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed. The 1901 Project works closely with local government, businesses, and other non-profit organizations to deliver a variety of programs and services to the community, with the ultimate goal of enhancing the quality of life for all members of the community.
