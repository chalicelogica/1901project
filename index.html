Empowering community through arts, culture, health, and technology

The 1901 Project is a collective of community leaders who are focused on improving community culture. Operating as a creative agency, the 1901 Project partners with local governance and local business to deliver experiences and free access resources to its community members. Acting as a marketing and event partner, the 1901 project collaborates with other non-profit organizations to improve community outreach.

Arts

education of Arts in the community through mentorship, workshops and industry resources

Culture

curating free access community events that highlight local culture and provide educational resources for the underprivileged.
 

health

providing health resrouces, free or low coast food programs.

Technolgy

improve financial literacy through access of new technologies including blockchain and cryptocurrency

About Us

1901 Project is California registered 501(c)3 Non-Profit organization focused on education and improvement of community culture through arts, music, mentorship, workshops and industry resources. Additionally, the 1901 Project is focused on financial literacy, through access to new technologies for low resource communities.
